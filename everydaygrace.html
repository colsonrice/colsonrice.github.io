Everyday Grace

Everyday Grace is a daily reflection and gratitude journaling app that provides inspirational or spiritual prompts. Users can input text (such as responses or journal entries) which is processed by a third-party AI service to generate helpful insights or suggestions. Rest assured, we do not store or link your text inputs to any personally identifiable information.
