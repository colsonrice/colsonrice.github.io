LDS Journal - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 3/27/25

Introduction

Thank you for using LDS Journal. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy by explaining how we handle the information you provide while using the app.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or request any personally identifiable information such as names, emails, or addresses. No account creation is required to use LDS Journal.

Text Input & AI Processing

Your journal entries or notes are sent to a third-party AI service for processing to generate spiritual or reflective prompts. These text inputs are not associated with any personally identifiable information and are not stored by us.

Aggregate Usage Data

We may view aggregated statistics, such as the total number of app downloads, but we do not track unique device identifiers or personal usage profiles.

Security Measures

While we do not store personal data, we use secure protocols when transmitting text to the third-party AI service.

Children’s Privacy

LDS Journal does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no user data is collected, the app is accessible to users of all ages. Parents or guardians with questions are welcome to contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be posted within the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at hello@squatchcraft.com.
