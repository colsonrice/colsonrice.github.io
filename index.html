The studio

One workbench in Indiana, apps and sites that punch above their weight.

Hi, I'm Colson. SquatchCraft is just me, building iOS apps and websites from a workbench in Indiana. I'm a Christian, a tinkerer, and on a good day a little funny. Most apps start with a real question I had myself, like how do I keep this reef alive, or can AI actually pick a decent lottery number, and they ship when I think they'd help somebody besides me. The websites are for small business owners I'd grab coffee with. Privacy-friendly, made with care, and hopefully a little fun to use.
