The Studio

One workbench in Michigan, a handful of apps that punch above their weight.

SquatchCraft is a one-person iOS studio. Each app is shipped because it answers a real question — how do I keep my reef from crashing, how do I quote this driveway, how do I keep a daily prayer practice. Native iOS, privacy-first, on-device when it can be, and built to feel polished without feeling precious.
