TankLab

Your all-in-one aquarium management app for iPhone.

TankLab helps you keep your aquarium healthy and thriving. Whether you're a beginner setting up your first tank or an experienced hobbyist managing a reef system, TankLab puts everything you need in one place.

Key Features

AI-Powered Assistant

Get personalized advice about water chemistry, fish compatibility, disease diagnosis, and more. The AI knows your tank's setup and gives tailored recommendations.

Test Strip Scanner

Snap a photo of your test strip next to the color chart and let AI read the values for you. Review, adjust, and save in seconds.

Water Parameter Tracking

Log pH, ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, GH, KH, temperature, and more. View trends over time with interactive charts and get alerts when values are out of range.

Maintenance Scheduler

Set up recurring tasks for water changes, filter cleaning, feeding, and dosing. Get notifications when tasks are due and track your maintenance streak.

Livestock Manager

Track every fish, invertebrate, and plant in your tank with species information and compatibility checks.

Tank Gallery & Journal

Document your tank's journey with photos and journal entries. Track progress, expenses, and milestones over time.

TankLab+

Upgrade to TankLab+ for unlimited AI questions, test strip scanning, iCloud sync across devices, and expanded tank and livestock limits.
